About UWU

UWU is a browser add-on made by a team of students from the University of Technology of Compiègne (in France)

Its aim is to collect data about the interactions made by real users on specific websites

This experimentation is only for education purpose and the collected data are only used by researchers and students

To remove UWU's authorizations to capture your interactions, you can simply disable UWU add-on form your browser's add-ons menu
